How to convert decimal to percent

1 = 100%

The value V% in percent (%) is equal to the decimal value Vd times 100%:

V% = Vd × 100%

Examples

0.01 = 0.01 × 100% = 1%

0.05 = 0.05 × 100% = 5%

0.3   = 0.3 × 100% = 30%

0.35 = 0.35 × 100% = 35%

3.5   = 3.5 × 100% = 350%
