How to convert centimeters to kilometers

1 kilometer is equal to 100000 centimeters:

1km = 100000cm

The distance d in kilometers (km) is equal to the distance d in centimeters (cm) divided by 100000:

d(km) = d(cm) / 100000

Example

Convert 20 centimeters to kilometers:

d(km) = 20cm / 100000 = 0.0002km

How many centimeters in a kilometer

One kilometer is equal to 100000 centimeter:

1km = 1km×100000 = 100000cm

How many kilometers in a centimeter

One centimeter is equal to 0.00001 kilometers:

1cm = 1cm/100000 = 0.00001km

How to convert 8 centimeters to kilometers

Divide 8 centimeters by 100000 to get kilometers:

8cm = 8cm/100000 = 0.00008km
