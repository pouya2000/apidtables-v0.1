How to convert feet to meters

1 foot is equal to 0.3048 meters:

1 ft = 0.3048 m

The distance d in meters (m) is equal to the distance d in feet (ft) times 0.3048:

d(m) = d(ft) × 0.3048

Example

Convert 2 feet to meters:

d(m) = 2ft × 0.3048 = 0.6096m
