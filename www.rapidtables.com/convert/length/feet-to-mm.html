How to convert feet to millimeters

1 foot is equal to 304.8 millimeters:

1 ft = 304.8 mm

The distance d in millimeters (mm) is equal to the distance d in feet (ft) times 304.8:

d(mm) = d(ft) × 304.8

Example

Convert 2 feet to millimeters:

d(mm) = 2ft × 304.8 = 609.6mm
