How to convert kilometers to miles

1 kilometer is equal to 0.62137119 miles:

1 km = (1/1.609344) mi = 0.62137119 mi

The distance d in miles (mi) is equal to the distance d in kilometers (km) divided by 1.609344:

d(mi) = d(km) / 1.609344

Example

Convert 20 kilometers to miles:

d(mi) = 20km / 1.609344 = 12.4274mi
