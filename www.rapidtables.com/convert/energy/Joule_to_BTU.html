How to convert Joules to BTU

1 J = 0.00094781712 BTUIT

1 BTUIT = 1055.05585262 J

The energy conversion of joules to BTU is given by the formula:

E(BTU) = E(J) / 1055.05585262 = 0.00094781712 ⋅ E(J)

 

For example: when E=500J,

E(BTU) = 500J / 1055.05585262 = 0.473909 BTU
