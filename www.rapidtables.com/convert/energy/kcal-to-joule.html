How to convert from kcal to joules

Thermochemical / food kilocalories to joules

1 kcalth = 4184 J

The energy in joules E(J) is equal to 4184 times the energy in thermochemical / food kilocalories E(kcal):

E(J) = 4184 × E(kcal-th)

Example

Convert 0.3 kcalth to joules.

E(J) = 4184 × 0.3 kcalth = 1255.2 J

International kilocalories to joules

1 kcalIT = 4186.8 J

The energy in joules E(J) is equal to 4186.8 times the energy in international kilocalories E(kcal-IT):

E(J) = 4186.8 × E(kcal-IT)

Example

Convert 0.3 kcalIT to joules.

E(J) = 4186.8 × 0.3kcalIT = 1256.04 J
