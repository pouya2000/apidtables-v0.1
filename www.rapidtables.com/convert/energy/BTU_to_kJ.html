How to convert BTU to Kilojoules

How to convert BTU (IT) to kilojoules:

1 BTUIT = 1.05505585262 kJ

So

E(kJ) = 1.05505585262 ⋅ E(BTU)

 

For example: when E=0.5 BTU,

E(kJ) = 1.05505585262 ⋅ 0.5 BTU  = 0.527528 kJ
