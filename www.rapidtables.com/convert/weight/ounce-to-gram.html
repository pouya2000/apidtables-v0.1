How to convert Ounces to Grams

1 ounce (oz) is equal to 28.34952 grams (g).

1 oz = 28.34952 g

The mass m in grams (g) is equal to the mass m in ounces (oz) times 28.34952:

m(g) = m(oz) × 28.34952

Example

Convert 5 oz to grams:

m(g) = 5 oz × 28.34952 = 141.7476 g
