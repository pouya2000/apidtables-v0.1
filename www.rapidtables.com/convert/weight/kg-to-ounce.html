How to convert Kilograms to Ounces

1 gram (kg) is equal to 35.27396195 ounces (oz).

1 kg = 35.27396195 oz

The mass m in ounces (oz) is equal to the mass m in kilograms (kg) divided by 0.02834952:

m(oz) = m(kg) / 0.02834952

Example

Convert 5 kg to ounces:

m(oz) = 5 kg / 0.02834952 = 176.37 oz
