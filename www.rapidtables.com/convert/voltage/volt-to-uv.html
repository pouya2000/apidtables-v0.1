How to convet volts to microvolts

1 V = 106 µV = 1000000 µV

or

1 µV = 10-6 V = 1/1000000 V

Volts to microvolts formula

The voltage V in microvolts (µV) is equal to the voltage V in volts (V) times 1000000:

V(µV) = V(V) × 1000000

Example

Convert 3 volts to microvolts:

V(µV) = 3V × 106 = 3000000 µV
