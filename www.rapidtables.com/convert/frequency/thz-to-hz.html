How to convert terahertz to hertz

1THz = 1012Hz

or

1Hz = 10-12THz

Terahertz to hertz formula

The frequency f in hertz (Hz) is equal to the frequency f in terahertz (THz) times 1012:

f(Hz) = f(THz) × 1012

Example

Convert 3 terahertz to hertz:

f(Hz) = 3THz × 1012 = 3×1012Hz
