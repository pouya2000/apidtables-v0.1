How to convert hertz to gigahertz

1Hz = 10-9GHz

or

1GHz = 109Hz = 1000000000Hz

Hertz to gigahertz formula

The frequency f in gigahertz (GHz) is equal to the frequency f in hertz (Hz) divided by 109:

f(GHz) = f(Hz) / 109

Example

Convert 300 hertz to gigahertz:

f(GHz) = 300Hz / 109 = 3×10-7GHz
