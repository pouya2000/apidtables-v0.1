1 MHz to kHz conversion

How to convert 1 megahertz (MHz) to kilohertz (kHz).

Calculation

The frequency f in kilohertz (kHz) is equal to 1 megahertz (MHz) times 1000:

f(kHz) = 1 MHz × 1000 = 1000 kHz

 

So 1 megahertz is equal to to 1000 kilohertz:

1 MHz = 1000 kHz
