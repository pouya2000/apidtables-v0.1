How to convert hertz to terahertz

1Hz = 10-12THz

or

1THz = 1012Hz

Hertz to terahertz formula

The frequency f in terahertz (THz) is equal to the frequency f in hertz (Hz) divided by 1012:

f(THz) = f(Hz) / 1012

Example

Convert 300 hertz to terahertz:

f(THz) = 300Hz / 1012 = 3×10-10THz
