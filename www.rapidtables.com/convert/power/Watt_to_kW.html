How to convert watts to kilowatts

1kW = 1000W

Watts to kilowatts conversion formula

The power in kilowatts P(kW) is equal to the power in watts P(W) divided by 1000:

P(kW) = P(W) / 1000

Example

Convert 1300W to kilowatts:

P(kW) = 1300W / 1000 = 1.3kW
