How to convert dBm to mW

The power conversion of dBm to mW is given by the formula:

P(mW) = 1mW ⋅ 10(P(dBm)/ 10)

 

So

1dBm = 1.258925mW

Example

Convert 13dBm to milliwatts:

P(mW) = 1mW ⋅ 10(13dBm/ 10) = 19.95mW
