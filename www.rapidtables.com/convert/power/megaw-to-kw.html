How to convert megawatts to kilowatts

1MW = 1000kW

1kW = 0.001MW

Megawatts to kilowatts conversion formula

The power in kilowatts P(kW) is equal to 1000 times the power in megawatts P(MW):

P(kW) = 1000 × P(MW)

Example

Convert 3MW to kilowatts:

P(kW) = 1000 × 3MW = 3000kW
