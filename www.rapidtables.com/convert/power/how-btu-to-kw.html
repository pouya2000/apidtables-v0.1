How to convert BTU/hr to kW

How to convert British Thermal Units per hour (BTU/hr) to kilowatts (kW).

One BTU/hr is equal to 0.00029307107 kilowatts:

1 BTU/hr = 0.00029307107 kW

BTU/hr to kilowatts conversion formula

The power in kilowatts P(kW) is equal to the power in BTUIT per hour P(BTU/hr) divided by 3412.142:

P(kW) = P(BTU/hr) / 3412.142

Example

Convert 20000 BTU/hr to kilowatts:

P(kW) = 20000 BTU/hr / 3412.142 = 5.861kW
