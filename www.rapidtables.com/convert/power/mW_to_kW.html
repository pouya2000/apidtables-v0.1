How to convert milliwatts to kilowatts

The power in kilowatts P(kW) is equal to the power in milliwatts P(mW) divided by one million:

P(kW) = P(mW) / 1000000

 

So

1mW = 0.000001kW

Example

Convert 300 milliwatts to kilowatts:

P(kW) = 300mW / 1000000 = 0.0003kW
