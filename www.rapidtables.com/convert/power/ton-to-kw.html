How to convert tons to kW

One refrigeration ton is equal to 3.5168525 kilowatts:

1 RT = 3.5168525 kW

One kilowatt is equal to 0.284345 refrigeration ton:

1 kW = 0.28434517 RT

 

So the power P in kilowatts (kW) is equal to the power P in refrigeration tons (RT) times 3.5168525:

P(kW) = P(RT) × 3.5168525

 

Example

Convert 2 RT to kilowatts:

P(kW) = 2 RT × 3.5168525 = 7.033705 kW
