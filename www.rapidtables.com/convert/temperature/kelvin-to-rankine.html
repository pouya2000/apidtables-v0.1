How to convert Kelvin to Rankine

The temperature T in degrees Rankine (°R) is equal to the temperature T in Kelvin (K) times 9/5:

T(°R) = T(K) × 9/5

Example

Convert 300 Kelvin to degrees Rankine:

T(°R) = 300K × 9/5 = 540 °R
