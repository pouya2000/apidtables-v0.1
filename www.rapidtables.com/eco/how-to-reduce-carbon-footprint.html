  • work Live close to work
    • Living close to work will reduce car usage and fuel consumption.
  • home Work from home
    • Working from home will reduce car usage and fuel consumption.
  • local_gas_station Buy a car with low fuel consumption
    • Smaller cars has lower fuel consumption.
  • directions_car Buy hybrid/electric car
    • Hybrid or electric cars transform the kinetic energy of the car to electric battery charge. Using the battery for electric motor drive will reduce fuel motor usage and fuel consumption.
  • directions_car Drive moderately
    • Avoid high acceleration / deceleration when driving.
    This will save your car and other cars fuel consumption, and also decrease the probability for car accidents. Low acceleration will enable electric motor usage instead of fuel motor in hybrid cars. Low deceleration will enable battery charge instead of breaks usage in hybrid cars.
  • directions_car Keep enough distance from next car
    • Keeping enough distance from next car will reduce unnecessary accelerations/deceleration and save fuel consumption.
  • directions_car When driving look ahead to avoid unnecessary accelerations and decelerations
  • directions_car Avoid driving with high motor RPM
  • directions_car Drive with highest gear possible
  • work Reduce baggage weight
  • close Close the car windows
  • access_time Avoid driving during rush hour
  • directions_car Avoid unnecessary car driving
  • directions_car Avoid motor idling
  • directions_car Keep tires with proper air pressure
  • settings Maintain your car regulary
  • map Plan your driving route to minimize the distance
  • directions_run Prefer stairs to elevator
  • directions_run Use the stairs instead of elevator
  • phone_iphone Use application to minimize driving time
    • Use application to minimize driving time/distance and save fuel. Waze application can be used to plan your driving route.
  • phone_iphone Use car pool application to reduce number of driving cars
    • Carpool is used to share a car drive with more than one person. Using carpool will reduce fuel consumption and reduce traffic.

kitchen Appliances

  • star_border Use Energy Star certified appliances
    • Check that the product you want to buy has ENERGY STAR label.
  • star_border Check appliance's Energy Efficiency rating
    • Check appliance's Energy Efficiency rating (A+++ is best). Try to look for the appliance rating in Amazon UK website. Currently Amazon US does not write the product's rating.
  • power Buy appliances with low power consumption
    • Check the appliance's power consumption.
  • settings_power Turn off appliances and gadgets that are in stand by state
    • Turing off appliances that are in stand by state will save electricity.
  • kitchen Avoid opening frequently the refrigerator door
    • Opening frequently the refrigerator's door will increase significatly the refrigirator's electricity consumption.
  • kitchen Leave enough space between the refrigerator and the wall to allow ventilation
    • Good refrigirator ventilation will reduce the electricity consumption.
  • local_laundry_service Wash your clothes in cold water
    • Washing clothes in cold water will avoid heating the water and reduce electricity consumption.
  • local_laundry_service Use shorter washing machine program
    • Short washing programs will reduce electricity consumption.
  • local_laundry_service Fill the washing machine / dryer / dishwasher before operation
    • Using washing machine that is full of clothes will reduce machine usage and save electricity.
  • tv Buy LED TV instead of plasma
    • Plasma TV has high power consumption and LED TV has low power consumption.
  • settings_brightness Reduce display brightness
    • Reduce TV/monitor/smartphone display brightness to reduce power consumption.
  • laptop Buy low power computer

    • Laptop computers or mini PC usually consume less power than desktop computers. Check the thermal design power (TDP) of the CPU and GPU and power performance. High performance CPU's and GPU's will consume more energy than low performance equivalents.

    Thermal design power (TDP) Power performance
  • laptop Buy computer with hight efficiency power supply
    • High efficiency power supply produce less heat and supply more power to the computer. Check the 80 Plus rating of the power supply unit (PSU). 80 Plus rating
  • phone_iphone Prefer smartphone over laptop
    • Smartphones consume less power than laptops.
  • settings_power Disconnect power supply unit when not used
    • TV, PC, monitor that have power supply unit (PSU) and charger have leak current. Unplugging or using an off switch may reduce power consumption and protect from surge currents.
  • cast_connected Prefer microwave oven over toaster oven
    • Microwave ovens consume less power than toaster ovens.
  • laptop Set PC energy saving features
    • Set energy saving features. Set time to turn off the display and time to put the computer to sleep. For Windows operating system select: Start > Settings > System > Power & sleep For MacOS select: Apple menu > System Prefrences > Energy Saver For iOS select: Settings > Display & Brightness: reduce the brightness and set Auto-Lock to 30 Seconds. For Android select: Settings > Display: Sleep with reduced sleep time and Brightness with reduced brightness.
  • battery_charging_full Set battery saver modes
    • Set Windows battery saver mode.
    Set Mac energy saver features.
    Set iPhone low power mode.
    Set Android battery.
    Turn off GPS location, WiFi and bluetooth when you don't need it.
  • local_laundry_service Use clothes drying rack instead of electric clothes dryer
    • Using clothes drying rack will reduce your elecricity consumption.
  • free_breakfast Put the exact amount of water that you need in your electric kettle
    • Using more water than you need will increase your elecricity consumption.
  • power Use electricity usage monitor
    • Electricity usage monitor will show you the power consumption and cost of every appliance that you use.
  • power Set dark mode
    • Modern monitors (e.g OLED) consume less power when display dark colors. Set browser and applications to Dark Mode.

lightbulb_outline Lighting

  • lightbulb_outline Use LED light bulbs
    • Incandescent light bulbs power consumption is 8 times bigger than equivalent LED light bulbs. fluorescent light bulbs power consumption is 2 times bigger than equivalent LED light bulbs. E.G: 7 watts LED light bulb equal 60 watts incandescent light bulb. Prefer warm while LED light bulbs with less blue color component.
  • lightbulb_outline Use low power light bulbs
    • Low power light bulbs have low wattage. Use 3-5 watts warm LED light bulbs.
  • lightbulb_outline Turn off the light when you leave the room
    • Turning of the light when you leave the room will reduce electricity consumption. Install presence detector to automatically turn off the light when leaving the room.
  • wb_sunny Use sunlight instead of artificial light
    • Open the curtains to allow sunlight to enter.
  • local_hotel Go to sleep early
    • Going to sleep early will reduce lighting usage.
  • format_paint Paint the walls white
    • Painting the walls white will increase light reflection and reduce required power consumption of the light bulbs.

restaurant Food

  • fastfood Reduce food consumption
    • Most people eat more than they need and throw a considerable amount of their food to the trash. Reducing food consumption will reduce the carbon emissions in the process of food production and transportation.
  • fastfood Reduce meat & dairy consumption
    • Meat and dairy production require large area of fields to grow food for the cows and sheeps. Growing crops to feed people directly, can produce much more food for a certain field.
  • local_drink Drink warm/cold drinks
    • In hot days drink cold water to get cold. In cold days drink hot water/drinks to get warm. This may reduce your electricity consumption for heating or cooling.
  • place Prefer locally produced food to imported food
    • Locally produced food requires less fuel consumption for transportation to your home.
  • nature Avoid food containing palm oil
    • Palm oil is mostly generated by heavy duty deforestation which reduce carbon storage by the trees.
  • local_gas_station Consider using biogas digester
    • Biogas is generated from food leftovers and organic waste and can be used for cooking and heating.
